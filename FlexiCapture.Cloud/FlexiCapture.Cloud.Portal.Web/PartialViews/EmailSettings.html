Instructions
Send in images and PDFs as e-mail attachments to auto-convert@datacapture.cloud and receive back conversion results by e-mail.

Supported attachments are (PDF, JPG, TIF, BMP, PCX, DCX, PNG, or archives ZIP, RAR, Z7). Total size of attachments should not exceed 30 MB. Results will be returned by a reply e-mail address to the 'from' sender's address.

Attachments will be processed with the default E-mail Conversion Profile conversion settings unless e-mail body contains one of the control tags listed below to overwrite settings for that one e-mail transaction. E-mail subject is ignored.
