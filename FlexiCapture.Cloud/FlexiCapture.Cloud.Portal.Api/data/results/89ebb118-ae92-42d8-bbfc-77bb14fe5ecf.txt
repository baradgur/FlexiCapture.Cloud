4. Terms of payment

4.1. Software development is paid by the
Customer within 5 days after signing the
Acceptance protocol.

4.2. Payment is made in USD by means of a
bank transfer from Customer account to Contractor
account under this contract and acceptance
protocol.

5. Rights to software

5.1. Exclusive rights to use software - both a
whole and its any part - belong to the Customer
from the time of software (or its relevant part)
creation.

5.2.  The Contractor has no right to use the
software (its source code or object code) as a whole
and any its part, except for creating another
software for the Customer.

5.3.  Moral rights to the Software belong to
individuals, by which the Software was created.

6. Responsibility of the parties

6.1.  Each party shall be liable to the amount of
damages, caused to the other party, for any failure
to perform the obligations under this Contract.

6.2. The Contractor shall pay to the Customer a
penalty in the amount of 0.15% of the Software
development cost for each day of delay in delivery
of the developed software.

6.3. The Customer shall pay to the Contractor a
forfeit for late payment of performed work in the
amount of 0.15% of unpaid sum for each calendar
day of delay.

6.4. The party, assumed any failure to perform the
obligations under this Contract, shall be exempt
from liability if it proves that such failure or
improper performance was caused by force
majeure, i.e. extraordinary and unavoidable
circumstances under the given conditions.

7. Settlement of disputes

7.1.  All  disputes and differences arising from
this    Contract    shall    be    resolved    through

negotiations.
